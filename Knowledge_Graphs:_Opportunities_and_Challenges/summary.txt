The document "Knowledge Graphs: Opportunities and Challenges" provides a comprehensive overview of the significance, opportunities, and challenges associated with knowledge graphs in the context of artificial intelligence (AI) and big data. Knowledge graphs, defined as graphs of data that accumulate and convey knowledge of the real world, have emerged as a crucial tool for representing complex information efficiently. The paper highlights the opportunities presented by knowledge graphs, such as enhancing AI systems' performance and their potential applications across various fields. However, it also delves into the technical challenges faced in the development of knowledge graphs, including issues like knowledge acquisition, graph embeddings, completion, fusion, and reasoning. The document emphasizes the importance of understanding both the opportunities and challenges associated with knowledge graphs to advance research and development in this field, shedding light on future directions for leveraging knowledge graphs effectively in AI systems and beyond.
